Atomic Habits

James Clear

Atomic Habits is a powerful self-improvement book that explains how small daily habits can create big success over time. James Clear teaches that tiny improvements made consistently lead to remarkable results. The book focuses on building good habits, breaking bad ones, and mastering the tiny behaviors that lead to extraordinary outcomes. It introduces the concept of the 1% improvement rule and explains practical strategies for habit formation.

Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert T. Kiyosaki

Rich Dad Poor Dad is a financial education book that compares two different mindsets about money. One father (Poor Dad) believes in working hard for money, while the other father (Rich Dad) believes in making money work for you. The book teaches important lessons about financial literacy, investing, assets, liabilities, and building wealth. It encourages readers to think differently about earning and managing money.
